Our Story

Sir C.R. Reddy Educational Institutions was founded in 1945 with a vision to provide quality education to students. Over the decades, we have grown into a premier educational group offering diverse programs from school level to postgraduate and professional education.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, quality education that develops informed, skilled, and responsible citizens capable of contributing to societal progress and global excellence.
